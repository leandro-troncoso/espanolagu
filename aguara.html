AGUARÁ was a personal project focused on selling printed clothing. In this venture, I was responsible for designing the apparel, as well as handling marketing, sales, after-sales service, and general management.



The brand image revolved around remastering the aesthetic of dark adolescence from the 2000s. We targeted a young-adult audience with a relaxed style. Animal motifs and themes of obscurantism/mysticism were heavily utilized. Cartoon imagery and fiery textures predominated. The designs aimed for emotion and impact.
